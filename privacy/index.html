Privacy Policy

All data generated by the use of the app remains solely on the user's device and is not transmitted to third parties by the app itself.

The app includes an optional feature that allows users to share their creations (drawings) with other applications of their choice. This sharing is initiated and controlled entirely by the user, and the app does not track or retain any shared content.

No analytics, advertising, or external tracking services are used. The app operates entirely locally, except for user-initiated sharing actions.

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@ryky.tech.
